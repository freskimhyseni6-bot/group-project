Our journey through the years

2009

Our journey began with SachPizza, a bold idea born in Kosovo: to craft honest, flavorful food that people don't just eat — but feel. What started as a single pizza shop quickly grew into a regional favorite, built on consistency, innovation, and heart.

2011

After the success of the first shop, Sach Pizza moved its location to a nearby store that was five times bigger than their initial shop. Shortly afterward, Sach Pizza opened its second store in the largest shopping mall in the city.

2015

Given the success of our first locations, we created a hybrid concept and opened the first location of Sachcaffé – integrating a whole new range of products that were fundamentally different from what Sach Pizza initially offered.

2017

SACH L.L.C. innovated by introducing its first hybrid concept that combined Sach Pizza and Sach Caffe under one roof. This concept was well-received and added a new dimension to the company's brand offerings, blending dining experiences in a unique way.

2018

The hybrid concept proved successful, leading to its introduction at the Royal Mall in Pristina, Kosovo. This expansion highlighted the brand's ability to adapt and innovate in different market environments.

2019

SACH L.L.C. took a significant step by going regional, opening its first city concept in the Republic of North Macedonia. This move marked the beginning of the company's regional expansion strategy, establishing its presence beyond Kosovo's borders.

2020

The company continued to grow by expanding with three new chains in North Macedonia and Kosovo. This expansion solidified SACH L.L.C.'s position as a leading regional player in the food and beverage industry.

2024
